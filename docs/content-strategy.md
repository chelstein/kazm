# KAZM Content Strategy

## KAZM 106.5 FM & 780 AM

## Mellow Mountain Radio

### Purpose

KAZM exists to serve listeners in Sedona, the Verde Valley, and Northern Arizona.

The website exists to extend the mission of the radio station.

The goal is not content volume.

The goal is not pageviews.

The goal is not SEO traffic.

The goal is to become the most trusted local information source in the region.

Trust is the product.

The radio signal is the product.

Everything else supports those goals.

---

# Content Mission

Every piece of content should accomplish at least one of the following:

* Inform listeners
* Protect listeners
* Connect listeners
* Entertain listeners
* Strengthen the community
* Support local businesses
* Reinforce KAZM's role as a trusted local voice

If content does not accomplish one of these goals, it should not exist.

---

# Content Pillars

## 1. Radio

The station is the center of everything.

Content should reinforce:

* Listen Live
* What's Playing
* Programming
* Featured Shows
* Coast to Coast AM
* Sports Broadcasts
* Special Programming

The station is not supporting the website.

The website is supporting the station.

---

## 2. Local News

Focus:

* Sedona
* Village of Oak Creek
* Cottonwood
* Clarkdale
* Camp Verde
* Jerome
* Cornville
* Rimrock
* Verde Valley
* Northern Arizona

News should emphasize:

* Public safety
* Local government
* Infrastructure
* Schools
* Community issues
* Economic development
* Local business

Local relevance always wins.

---

## 3. Weather

Weather is a core service.

Coverage should prioritize:

* Current conditions
* Forecasts
* Heat safety
* Travel impacts
* Monsoon impacts
* Wildfire conditions
* Road conditions
* Air quality

Weather should be treated as a primary destination.

Not a secondary widget.

---

## 4. Sports

Coverage should prioritize:

### KAZM Broadcast Sports

* Arizona Cardinals
* Arizona Diamondbacks
* Phoenix Suns
* Arizona State Athletics
* Northern Arizona University Athletics

### Community Sports

* Sedona Red Rock High School
* Mingus Union High School
* Camp Verde High School
* Youth athletics
* Local tournaments

Sports should connect listeners to their teams and their community.

---

## 5. Community Events

The station should become the primary source for:

* Festivals
* Fundraisers
* Community meetings
* Arts events
* Nonprofit events
* Local celebrations
* Public gatherings

Events content should strengthen community participation.

---

## 6. Local Business

Local businesses are part of the community fabric.

Coverage may include:

* Openings
* Expansions
* Anniversaries
* Community involvement
* Economic impact

Avoid disguised advertising.

Maintain editorial integrity.

---

# Editorial Standards

Every piece of content should answer:

Why should someone living in Northern Arizona care?

If local relevance cannot be established, reconsider publishing.

---

# Public Service Priority

Public service information always takes precedence.

Examples:

* Wildfires
* Evacuations
* Road closures
* Severe weather
* Public safety notices
* Amber Alerts
* Emergency information

In emergencies:

Public information overrides normal content priorities.

---

# Voice & Tone

KAZM should sound:

* Friendly
* Trustworthy
* Informative
* Helpful
* Authentic
* Community-oriented

KAZM should not sound:

* Corporate
* Academic
* Sensational
* Political
* Combative
* Click-driven

The tone should feel like a trusted local broadcaster.

---

# Headline Standards

Headlines should inform.

Not manipulate.

Good:

* Sedona Approves New Trail Access Plan
* Highway 89A Closed Following Rockslide
* Cardinals Open Season Sunday

Bad:

* Residents Shocked By New Decision
* This Arizona Story Is Going Viral
* You Won't Believe What Happened Next

If it sounds like YouTube clickbait, rewrite it.

---

# Photography Strategy

Prefer:

* Real station photography
* Real local photography
* Real events
* Real people
* Real businesses
* Real landscapes

Avoid:

* AI-generated people
* Generic stock photography
* Fake imagery
* Corporate stock photos

Authenticity matters.

---

# SEO Philosophy

Write for listeners first.

Search engines second.

Community value matters more than keyword density.

Trust matters more than rankings.

---

# Anti-Slop Rules

Never publish:

* Clickbait
* Ragebait
* Celebrity gossip
* AI filler content
* Generic national news summaries
* Manufactured controversy
* Empty content

Never sacrifice:

* Trust
* Accuracy
* Credibility
* Community value

for traffic.

---

# The Morning Coffee Test

Before publishing any content ask:

Would someone discuss this over coffee tomorrow morning?

Would a local resident care?

Would a local business owner care?

Would a listener mention this on-air?

Does this help someone understand their community?

If the answer is no:

Do not publish it.

---

# The KAZM Standard

Every article.

Every page.

Every event.

Every forecast.

Every sports story.

Every community update.

Should reinforce one idea:

KAZM 106.5 FM & 780 AM is the trusted voice of Sedona, the Verde Valley, and Northern Arizona.

Protect that trust at all costs.
