# KAZM Information Architecture

## Purpose

The KAZM website exists to help listeners quickly access the station, local information, weather, sports, programming, events, and contact information.

The site should be organized around listener needs, not internal departments or design trends.

Primary goal:

Help visitors understand what is happening in Sedona right now and make it easy to listen live.

---

# Primary Navigation

Use this main navigation:

1. Listen Live
2. News
3. Weather
4. Sports
5. Schedule
6. Events
7. Advertise
8. About
9. Contact

Navigation should be simple, obvious, and consistent across the site.

Avoid hidden, clever, or experimental navigation.

---

# Homepage Structure

The homepage is the front porch of the station.

Above the fold, users should see:

1. KAZM Logo
2. Listen Live
3. Now Playing
4. Current Weather
5. Top Local Story
6. Emergency Alert if active

The homepage should answer:

"What is happening in Sedona right now?"

---

# Homepage Sections

Recommended homepage order:

1. Hero / Listen Live Area
2. Now Playing
3. Weather
4. Local News
5. Sports
6. Programming Schedule Preview
7. Community Events
8. Advertise With KAZM
9. About KAZM
10. Footer

No unnecessary marketing sections should appear above listener utility content.

---

# Page Map

## `/`

Homepage.

Primary purpose:

* Listen live
* See what is playing
* Check weather
* View top local news
* See sports and events

## `/listen`

Dedicated listening page.

Should include:

* Stream player
* Now playing
* Recently played
* Programming schedule preview
* Station contact information

## `/news`

Local news index.

Should include:

* Top stories
* Public safety updates
* Local government
* Community news
* Business news
* Arts and culture

## `/news/[slug]`

Individual news article.

Should include:

* Headline
* Date
* Location
* Summary
* Article body
* Related stories
* Local context

## `/weather`

Weather page.

Should include:

* Current conditions
* Forecast
* Active alerts
* Heat information
* Travel conditions
* Wildfire weather if applicable

## `/sports`

Sports index.

Should include:

* Arizona Cardinals
* Arizona Diamondbacks
* Phoenix Suns
* ASU Athletics
* NAU Athletics
* Local high school sports
* Upcoming broadcasts

## `/schedule`

Programming schedule.

Should include:

* Daily schedule
* Weekly schedule
* Show descriptions
* Coast to Coast AM
* Sports broadcasts
* Special programming

## `/events`

Community events.

Should include:

* Upcoming events
* Community announcements
* Local festivals
* Public meetings
* Submission information

## `/advertise`

Advertise with KAZM.

Should include:

* Why advertise locally
* Audience overview
* Advertising options
* Sponsorship opportunities
* Contact form

Tone should be consultative, not aggressive.

## `/about`

About KAZM.

Should include:

* Station identity
* Mellow Mountain Radio positioning
* Community role
* Broadcast information
* Service area

## `/contact`

Contact page.

Should include:

* General contact
* Advertising contact
* News tips
* Event submissions
* Station phone
* Email
* Location if appropriate

# Content Priority

Across the site, prioritize:

1. Listen Live
2. Now Playing
3. Emergency Information
4. Weather
5. Local News
6. Programming Schedule
7. Sports
8. Events
9. Advertising
10. About

Everything else is secondary.

---

# Emergency Information Architecture

The site must support emergency mode.

Emergency alerts may appear:

* At the top of the homepage
* In the site header
* On the weather page
* On relevant news pages

Emergency content may override normal homepage order.

Examples:

* Wildfires
* Evacuations
* Road closures
* Severe weather
* Public safety notices
* Amber Alerts

---

# Mobile Information Architecture

Mobile users should be able to reach core actions quickly.

Persistent or highly visible mobile actions:

* Listen Live
* Weather
* News
* Schedule

Primary mobile buttons should be large, clear, and easy to tap.

Avoid burying the stream inside menus.

---

# Footer Structure

Footer should include:

* KAZM logo
* Listen Live
* News
* Weather
* Sports
* Schedule
* Events
* Advertise
* About
* Contact
* FCC / public file links if applicable
* Copyright

Footer should be useful, not decorative.

---

# Content Rules

Do not create pages unless they serve a real listener need.

Avoid:

* Empty landing pages
* Generic feature pages
* Startup-style sections
* Fake content
* Placeholder content
* Unused routes

Every page must have a clear purpose.

---

# Final Test

Before adding a page, route, section, or navigation item, ask:

Does this help listeners?

Does this support the station?

Does this make KAZM easier to use?

Would a longtime Sedona listener understand why this exists?

If no, do not add it.
